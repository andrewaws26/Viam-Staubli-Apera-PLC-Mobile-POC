## Recurring recommendation (4th time)

1. **Check .prog files, not just .capture files** — data_manager syncs and deletes .capture files immediately, so they rarely exist on disk
2. **Grace period after viam-server restarts** — suppress alerts for 60s post-restart
3. **Filter startup-transient errors** from error rate calculations
4. **Hysteresis** — require 2+ consecutive failed checks before alerting
5. **Check viam-server uptime** — if uptime < 5 min, don't alert on capture gaps
